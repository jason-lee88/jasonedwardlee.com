About Me

A look into my background...

Hello! My name is Jason Lee and I'm currently a senior at Lehigh University in Bethlehem, PA studying computer science.

At Lehigh, I've been involved with campus organizations such as being the Founder and President of the Lehigh Tea Club and Secretary for Residence Hall Council. I am also a work-study employee through the university as a Lab Monitor for the Computer Science & Engineering Deptartment.

Previously, I grew up in Stroudsburg, PA and graduated from Stroudsburg High School in 2018. During my time in high school, I explored many activities and programs the school offered such as being the President of the Science Olympiad team, Vice President of Band, Future Business Leaders of America, JV Cross Country, Environmental Club, and the Chess Team. Additionally, I have worked in retail with Best Buy, Five Below, and volunteered at Lehigh Valley Hospital-Pocono.

These experiences have lead to the growth of my personal development in many areas such as empathy, communication, problem-solving, decision-making, critical thinking, teamwork, patience, and time management.

In my free time aside from my schoolwork, I enjoy experimenting with programming, listening to music, playing video and board games, socializing and seeing/meeting other people, and living life to its fullest. Ultimately in life, my goal is to positively impact the world in as many ways as I can both technologically and emotionally.


My fascination with programming and development came from my early teen years when I stumbled upon Roblox, an online game creation platform that hosts "immersive user-generated 3D worlds." After traversing the many games that users across the platform have created, I decided I wanted to try my hand at making one. I absorbed myself in online tutorials and experimentation with their development environment to learn and understand Roblox's object-oriented implementation of the scripting language Lua. During this time, I discovered my passion for creation and algorithmic problem solving. Eventually, my experience with Roblox's game creation environment led me to develop a simple economic simulator game on the platform.
